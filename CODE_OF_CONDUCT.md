# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions, and focus on technical outcomes.
- Give and accept feedback professionally.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal attacks, intimidation, or doxxing.
- Deliberate disruption or bad-faith participation.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and may take appropriate action in response to behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Reporting

To report an incident, contact: `butch@slowbern.dev`

All reports will be reviewed and handled as confidentially as possible.
